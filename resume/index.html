What I've Been Up To

Project Manager, Development and Training Services, Inc.

Mr. Jessee serves as Data Entry/Analysis Manager for U.S. Department of State’s Office of Global Change (EGC) in the Bureau of Oceans and International Environmental and Scientific Affairs (OES) Formative Evaluation Support to the Global Climate Change Initiative. As a member of the evaluation team, Mr. Jessee is responsible for supporting Senior Evaluation Specialist and conducting Data Quality Assessments (DQAs) for data collected on 50 current interagency agreements and grants using DoS’s Standard Foreign Assistance Indicators. The evaluation team is also responsible for collecting and tracking quantitative and qualitative data, supporting collection and verification of performance data and target setting for DoS’s annual Performance Plan and Report (PPR), identifying, corroborating, collecting and sharing success stories to illustrate bright spots in this effort, supporting the completion of Performance Monitoring Plans for recipients, and preparing the final report.

Sales Coordinator, NCC Media

Research and Finance Assistant, VaxTrac

Delivered resource-reallocation recommendations as appropriate to research director based on accurate budget and expense reviews. Edited and proofread marketing materials and other documents. Homelessness Prevention Counselor, Housing Counseling Services

Administered Emergency Rental Assistance Program (ERAP) and Homelessness Prevention and Rapid Re-Housing Program (HPRP) to clients in the District of Columbia of 40-year-old non-profit organization that provides comprehensive housing counseling, training, advocacy, technical assistance, and housing opportunities for low- and moderate-income homebuyers, homeowners, and tenants. Made informational presentations about both programs to clients, determined client eligibility, processed applications, and evaluated client documentation. Communicated final decisions to clients and landlords. Logged and recorded all financial and other information for record keeping and accounting purposes.

Policy Intern, National Public Radio

Drafted, edited, and submitted letters signed by then-CEO Vivian Schiller to members of Congress whose districts/states were being impacted. Scheduled follow-up meetings convening NPR's Director of Public Policy and Legislation, Director of Government Relations, Vice-President of Government Relations, and staff members in respective Congresspersons' offices. Supported NPR's advocacy conference calls with representatives of member stations to coordinate this successful project.
